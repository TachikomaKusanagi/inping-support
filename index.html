About iNping

iNping is a network diagnostic tool for iOS featuring ping, traceroute, port scanning, DNS lookup, and whois functionality.

Privacy Policy

iNping does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All network diagnostic operations are performed locally on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the ping feature?

Enter a domain name or IP address in the Host field and tap Start. Results will appear in real-time.

Why am I getting "Host unreachable" errors?

This is normal for hosts that don't respond to ping requests, are offline, or block ICMP traffic.

Does the app work on cellular networks?

Yes, all features work on both Wi-Fi and cellular connections.

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • Internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)
