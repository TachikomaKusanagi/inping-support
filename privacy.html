Privacy Policy for iNping

Last updated: June 2025

Information We Collect

iNping does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

Network Operations

All network diagnostic operations (ping, traceroute, port scanning, DNS lookup, whois) are performed locally on your device and do not involve data collection.

Third Party Services

iNping does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

No user data is stored on external servers. All operations are local to your device.
